About YA Gateway

My name is Ben Wilkoff, and I love YA fiction.

I wanted a way to recommend my favorite YA novels as I was reading them. I wanted a way to add each book to a growing list, something that I wish I had growing up.

This site is the direct result of that want. Each book has a short recommendation from me, and then the rest of the resources are pulled from GoodReads and Amazon. This site has affiliate links. Take that for what it is worth. I have read each and every one of these books and I highly recommend them to anyone who needs a way in to understanding themselves or one another.
